Overview

Direct Cars. Ltd. is a professional pre-owned cars dealership aimed at providing Quality certified Used Cars under the Brand Umbrella of Carnation Certified Pre-Owned Cars. Our aim is to make buying & selling used cars and joyful experience. Direct Cars assurance, Great Quality, Great Experience, Great Value
